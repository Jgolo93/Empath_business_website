1 Project Overview

Glen's Grass is a small lawn care and garden maintenance business operating in Cape Town. Like many local service businesses, they relied entirely on word-of-mouth referrals and had no online presence. The owner needed a way to attract new customers, manage bookings, and look professional — all on a tight budget.

"I never thought I could afford a proper website. Empath built everything I needed in one weekend, and it cost less than my monthly phone bill."

2 Client Requirements

The client needed a simple but professional solution that would:

  • Showcase services and pricing clearly
  • Allow customers to request quotes online
  • Provide contact information and service areas
  • Look professional and trustworthy
  • Work well on mobile phones
  • Include a business email address
  • Be easy to update and maintain

3 Technical Stack

To keep costs low while delivering quality, we used:

Technology Choices

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (vanilla)

Backend: Flask (Python) for dynamic content

Database: SQLite for customer inquiries

Hosting: Shared hosting (R50/month)

Domain: .co.za domain (R99/year)

Email: Google Workspace (R70/month)

4 Development Process

We completed the entire project in 48 hours over a weekend:

Saturday (Day 1):

  • Morning: Client consultation and requirements gathering
  • Afternoon: Design mockup and approval
  • Evening: Basic HTML structure and styling

Sunday (Day 2):

  • Morning: Backend development and database setup
  • Afternoon: Contact forms and quote request system
  • Evening: Testing, deployment, and email setup

5 Key Features

The final website includes:

  • Professional Design: Clean, modern layout that works on all devices
  • Service Pages: Detailed descriptions of lawn care services
  • Pricing Information: Transparent pricing packages
  • Quote Request Form: Easy way for customers to get estimates
  • Contact Information: Phone, email, and service area details
  • Photo Gallery: Before/after photos of work completed
  • Customer Testimonials: Social proof and trust building
  • Business Email: Professional @glensgrass.co.za email address

6 Results & Impact

Within the first month of launching:

  • 25+ quote requests through the website
  • 15 new customers acquired online
  • 40% increase in monthly revenue
  • Professional appearance improved customer trust
  • Time savings from automated quote collection
