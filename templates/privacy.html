Privacy Policy

At Empath IT Support, I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use my services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access my services.

1. Information I Collect

I may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using my services, including:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device information (make, model, serial numbers)
  • Issue descriptions and service requirements
  • Payment information (for billing purposes only)
  • Service history and technical details
— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

2. How I Use Your Information

I use your personal information to:

  • Provide IT support and technical services
  • Communicate about service updates and progress
  • Process payments and billing
  • Maintain service records and history
  • Improve my services and customer experience
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

3. Information Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. I may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services necessary for payment processing or technical support
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Safety: To protect rights, property, or personal safety
— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

4. Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure storage of client records and technical documentation
  • Encrypted communication channels for remote support
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security updates and system maintenance
  • Secure disposal of old parts and storage media

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

5. Data Retention

I retain your personal information only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing ongoing support and warranty services
  • Legal and regulatory compliance
  • Business record-keeping requirements
  • Resolving disputes and troubleshooting

Service records are typically retained for 3 years, after which they are securely destroyed.

— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
— ✦ ✦ ✦ —

  • Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.
